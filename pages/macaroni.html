Macaroni Salad

Recipe by Graden

Macaroni Salad is a classic appetizer. It is easy-to-make and loved by many. This recipe takes 20 minutes of preparation time, 10 minutes of cooking time, and at least 4 hours for refrigeration. After such, you can delight the appetite of 10 people!

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup distilled white vinegar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 stalks chopped celery
  • 1 large chopped onion
  • 1/2 seeded and chopped green bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot

Directions:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook elbow macaroni in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Rinse under cold water and drain.
  2. Mix mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl; stir in celery, onion, green pepper, carrot, pimentos, and macaroni.
  3. Refrigerate salad for at least 4 hours before serving, but preferably overnight.
